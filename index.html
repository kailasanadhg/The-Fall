The Fall

by Kailasanadh G

The words of the shadowed one ring,
when silence sounds loud

In the mist, where the man 
who once stood strong fell down,

Beneath the iron will, beneath the auroma,
A weak heart throbs
like a violin string yet to be touched

The path to abyss feels clear,
Yet the one that suffered know
the scars they set aside

They speak  of the forger of wrath,
who shaped the war - of guilt, of pride, of rage

Not knowing the war itself
lies in your own tangled mind,
waiting to be unravelled, to feel tranquil again 
from the conflict of life itself.
